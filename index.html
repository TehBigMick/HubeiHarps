About the club

Proudly representing Gaelic games in Wuhan

Hubei Harps is an Irish GAA club based in Wuhan, China, bringing together players, supporters, and friends through Gaelic football, hurling, and a strong club identity.

We are a home for Irish expats, local players, and anyone curious about trying one of Ireland’s best sporting traditions. You do not need previous experience. If you are willing to train, learn, and get stuck in, you will fit right in.
