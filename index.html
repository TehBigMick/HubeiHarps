About the Club

Hubei Harps is a Wuhan-based Irish GAA club bringing people together through Gaelic games. We are building a strong community around Gaelic football, hurling, and shared club spirit. Whether you have played for years or have never picked up a ball before, you are welcome here.

Our aim is simple: train hard, represent the club proudly, and give people in Wuhan a proper chance to experience Irish sport and community.
